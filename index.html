Welcome to RL Fasteners Manufacturer of quality high tensile fasteners

R L FASTENERS Company is the result of tireless efforts of many minds and standing on so high ethical values. The company engaged in the manufacturing and supply of High Tensile & Stainless Steel Fastener as per standards. The organization has technical professionals staff having well experience in fasteners manufacturing field and good potential knowledge in industrial engineering field. The prime motto of company is to research new methods and techniques to enhance quality of the fastener products to meet the expectations of the customers.

The products manufactured are forged from cold headed quality stainless steel. We manufacture fasteners in DIN, IS, ISO and BS standards. We are equipped with latest technology machines, tools and testing instruments, technical knowledge and manpower for manufacturing of fasteners. All manufacturing facilities are supported with an online quality assurance system employing statistical process control. Each fastener lot is marked with code numbers for traceability of origin to the steel mill.

It’s our aim to built long-lasting business associations with our estimated clients, by providing them a product that stands as testimony to our reliability, commitment and excellence.

PRODUCTS

Our diversified range of fasteners includes half and fully threaded Hexagon head bolts, Hexagon head nuts, Allen bolts, screws with half and full thread and washers.

The fasteners are proposed for the use in high pressure and high temperature, high tensile, corrosive and underwater applications. We manufacture as per customer’s required specifications and under third party inspection of their choices.

A wide range of standard cold forged high tensile fasteners over 100 varieties covering the diameter range of M3 to M16 and the length range of 6mm to 150 mm.

Standard products cover most of international standards DIN, IS, ISO, BS and engineered as per respective standards.

These products cover a very wide range of industries viz. Automobile sector, standard / special m/c building sector, textile sector, marine sector, printing machines, pharmacy equipments etc.
